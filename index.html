About Thermolink

Thermolink was born out of a profound inspiration from a Dutch farmer who leveraged excess heat generated by Bitcoin mining to grow tulips. This ingenious approach sparked our vision to create a sustainable, circular economy that closes the loop on energy waste.

Our startup takes excess heat from data processors in modern data centers and redistributes it to urban homes and neighborhoods in Berlin. Just as the Dutch farmer turned a byproduct of technology into a thriving environment for flowers, we aim to turn wasted energy into a life-enhancing resource for cities.
